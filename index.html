About

From zero to exit —
and still building.

I'm a product leader, entrepreneur, and AI advocate based in Tampere, Finland. In 2018, I co-founded FabricAI alongside Jarkko Tolvanen, Juhani Tolvanen, and Kaarle Putti — driven by a shared frustration with the manual, time-consuming work of purchase invoice processing.

We built FabricAI from a whiteboard sketch into a product trusted by over 40,000 companies, processing more than 1 million invoices every month. Our ambition was clear from day one — we literally drew "Exit to Visma" on a whiteboard. In 2023, that vision became reality.

Today I lead FabricAI's product line as Product Director at Visma, driving the business from 1M€ ARR toward 5M€. Alongside that, I invest in and support early-stage founders through my holding company, Calmbay Capital Oy.
